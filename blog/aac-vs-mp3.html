Our Recommendation in 2025

Use AAC if: - You mainly use modern devices (iPhone, Android, Mac, Windows 10+) - You want the best possible quality/size ratio - You’re okay with slightly lower compatibility on ancient devices

Use MP3 if: - You need 100% compatibility (old cars, cheap players, etc.) - You share files with others

Our converter defaults to AAC @ 320 kbps because it’s simply the best choice for 99% of users in 2025.

MP3 won the 2000s. AAC (and Opus) won the 2020s.
