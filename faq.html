Frequently Asked Questions

Is my file uploaded to a server?

No. All conversion happens in your browser using JavaScript. Your file never leaves your device.

Which formats are supported?

Input: MP4, MOV, WEBM, OGG
Output: MP3 (128kbps), WAV

Can I use this on mobile?

Yes! Works on iOS and Android. Install as a PWA for offline use.

Is this free?

100% free. No signup, no limits, no watermarks.

Why are there ads?

Ads help cover hosting costs. You can support us by using the tool!
